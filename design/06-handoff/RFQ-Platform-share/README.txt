RFQ Platform — HTML Prototype
=============================

HOW TO VIEW
-----------
Just double-click "index.html" to open it in any browser (Chrome/Edge recommended).

An internet connection is needed the first time — the page loads two things
online: the Geist font (Google Fonts) and the ECharts chart library. Everything
else (logos, app code) is bundled in this folder.

FOLDER CONTENTS
---------------
  index.html    - the prototype (open this)
  support.js    - app logic / interactions
  assets/       - image assets
      rangsons-logo2.png   - Rangsons logo MARK (square icon, used in the sidebar)
      rangsons_logo.png    - Rangsons full WORDMARK (used on the login screen)

Keep all files together — moving index.html out of this folder will break the
logos and interactions.
