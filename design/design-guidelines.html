Why this works

  • Branded surface — the gradient anchors identity; the content stays neutral.
  • Scannable groups — overlines (Workspace / Admin) chunk the nav.
  • Status, not just links — count badges (42 active, 3 need attention) make it a glance-able health bar.
  • Clear active state — filled pill + left accent bar + glow.
  • Role context — user card at the bottom shows who/what role.
  • Collapsible — 64px icon rail with tooltips for power users.
  • Role-filtered — items a role can't use don't render.

Brand logos — two lockups

Horizontal lockup (rangsons_logo.png) — top bar (expanded), Login / Landing, wide headers, documents/PDF.

Emblem mark (rangsons-logo2.png) — favicon, collapsed sidebar rail, avatar / app icon, compact & mobile.

Both anchor the brand indigo #2E3192 + red #EC1D23. On dark/gradient surfaces, place a logo on a white chip (as in the sidebar) or use a white/mono version (asset to be produced).
